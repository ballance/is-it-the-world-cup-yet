Embed the Countdown

Drop a live World Cup 2026 countdown into your blog, sidebar, or fan page with one iframe. It counts down to the next kickoff, switches to the live score during matches, and goes quiet when the tournament ends. No tracking, no third-party scripts, no API keys.

Copy this

<iframe src="https://worldcupyet.com/embed/countdown.html"
        width="320" height="140"
        style="border:0;border-radius:10px"
        title="World Cup 2026 countdown"
        loading="lazy"></iframe>

Sizing

  • Responsive from 240×100 up to a full-width 800×220; the type rescales to fit, no per-site tuning needed.
  • Default 320×140 fits most blog sidebars cleanly.
  • For a banner-style placement, try width="100%" and height="160".

What it shows

  • Before kickoff — the next scheduled match and a live countdown 3d 4h, 2h 15m, or 4m 30s.
  • During a match — a pulsing ● LIVE tag, the team names, and the live score.
  • After the final — a trophy and See you in 2030.

Polling and bandwidth

  • matches.json is fetched once on load and cached aggressively.
  • live.json is polled every 30 seconds only while at least one match is in its live window. Outside that window, the widget is silent on the network.
  • The countdown ticks locally every second — no requests.
